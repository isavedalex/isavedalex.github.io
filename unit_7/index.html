JBJ Studio

JBJ Studio is a commercial recording studio based in Uxbridge, West London. We pride ourselves in offering industry standard results at an affordable price.

Services

Our facility is completely soundproofed and acoustically treated by Graham Whitehead of Ethos Acoustics. We offer a full range of services including writing, pre-production, recording, mixing, mastering, sound correction and voiceover for TV/Film/Game. For the most competitive prices in West London and a professional sounding outcome get in touch today

Equipment

The heart of our West London recording studio is based around a vintage Amek Matchless console and a full pro-tools rig connected by short, high quality cable runs into our Lavry AD10 converters. Monitoring is provided by PMC Tb2, Yamaha NS10m and KRK 7000b. The engineers at JBJ Studio understand the importance of mic pre amps and we're proud to offer our clients a choice between four focusrite red1's, 26 x AML 1081 Neve Clone and two Drawmer 1960 tube pre's. The microphone cupboard boasts a broad supply of Neumann, AKG, Shure, SE and Audio-Technica. We combine the best of analogue with the best of digital.

Engineers

Jamie Brown

Jamie has over 10 years of first hand experience engineering and mixing records at the top level, achieving multiple national and regional radio playlists for artists. Working with high profile musicians and producers in the best studios in the UK and all over the world has resulted in him working with Lucy Rose, Razorlight and Scouting for girls. As JBJ's longest serving engineer and producer he has gained a thorough knowledge of both rooms and how to work successfully within them. Jamie has worked with producers such as Chris Potter, John Mitchell, Ed Buller, Tim Rowkins and Stuart Epps.

Alex Crowley

Alex is a graduate of Music Technology from Amersham & Wycombe college and has experience in recording and producing bands from a variety of styles. Having played in punk rock and indie bands in the past a unique understanding between bands of these stlyes has been developed and previous artists worked with include Hang the Bastard, Since May and Ethan Beer. As a long time user of JBJ studio he has a thorough knowledge of the studio and access to a range of musicians to collaborate with on your next project.
