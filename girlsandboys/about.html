Girls & Boys runs unique monthly events that give a voice to every artist booked, rather than focusing on giving support or headline titles respectively.

The monthly events aim to present new artists on an equal footing in order to subvert the norm, providing something that would break down genre barriers and bring together exciting new artists on the same bill.
