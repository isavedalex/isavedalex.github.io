My Work

OmniPhoto

Collaborative Group Photo Sharing

OmniPhoto was a concept project inspired by the frustrations of people looking to combine all of their photographs from an event together with other people. I used the entire UX spectrum during the project, including User Researching, Prototyping, Design and User Testing.

About Me

I'm a fanatic for problem-solving, and I believe even the most complex problems can be solved with great design. I started my career in customer service and sales before transitioning into UX design, and I excel in empathising with people to really get to the heart of a problem. I'm a keen musician and photographer and am also well versed in HTML 5 and CSS 3.
