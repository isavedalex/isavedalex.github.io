ABOUT

The Buzz Lightweights are a high energy, fun filled, wedding and party band. With a wide repertoire ranging from 50's to 00's classics, keeping the young and old on the dance floor all night.


Our 6 piece line-up consists of drums, bass, guitars, keyboards, sax, trumpet and vocals. The band features members of the BBC concert orchestra, West end theatre shows and professional studio players.


What stands the Buzz Lightweights above the average band is their inclusive style and their ability to read and interact with the audience.


The show always looks and sounds great with a lighting and sound rig to suit all types of venue.


We can supply a DJ style playlist, during and after our set at no extra charge removing the need for a DJ but ensuring the party keeps its vibe and energy.


We're happy to accomodate early set up times to facilitate speeches etc.. If live background music is required during dinner this can also be arranged.


Based in West London, and available to travel nationwide. Travel is included in our price for up to 1.25 hours from our base.
